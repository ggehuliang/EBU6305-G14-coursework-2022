Visitor imformation

First, information for free open of Shaanxi History Museum

Admission is free, every Tuesday to Sunday


Visiting time

Winter
(15th Nov.-14th Mar., next year)

9.00 am - 17.30 pm

(Box office closes at 16.00 pm)


Summer
(15th Mar.-14th Nov.)

8.30 am - 18.00 pm

(Box office closes at 16.30 pm)


It is closed all day on Mondays for refurbishment(except national legal holidays)


Second,implementing the principle of"no charge but not no tickets"


Third,there are free deposit boxes in the museum, please keep the valuables by yourself.


Fourth, visiting vehicles must part in specified parking lots in order, please do not park in the non-parking area.


Fifth,in the exhibition hall,flash photographing,eating and drinking,noise and frolic are prohibited, doing business unrelated to the museum visit is banned;behaviors such as spitting,smoking and littering are also forbidden.


Sixth,please take good care of the cultural relics,exhibition facilities,other public service facilities, flowers,plants,green space and so on; if you damage them, you should be liable for the damageand legal responsibility.


Seventh,museum services charge in accordance with the price standards set by price department. Charging items include:the temporary exhibition held through the market operation,display interpretation,the audio guide rent,parking,souvenirs,the book sales,catering services and so on;the audience can choose those services according to their own needs.


Eighth,the Shaanxi History Museum is responsible for the explanation of the management methods.

Telephone:029-85253806, the Shaanxi History Museum May, 2022

How to get there

Shaanxi History Museum is located at 91 Xiaozhai East Road, Xi'an
