With a handle in the shape of a horse head, this sword is a gem of a collection of articles for daily use from a minority nationality of the Shang Dynasty, called Gui Fang. This kind of artifact has also been excavated in Qinglong of Hebei Province, Shilou of Shanxi Province,the Fuhao’s tomb of Yinxu Ruins at Anyang, Henan Province. Using the head of a snake, horsesheep as the handle of a knife or sword is the decorative feature of this nomadic tirbe from Northern China.
